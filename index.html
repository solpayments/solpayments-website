SolPayments is a payment processing platform for crypto-currencies that allows individuals and businesses of all sizes to accept payments on their websites, online shops, and decentralized apps.

Our overarching goal is to make it possible for anyone from anywhere in the world to set up shop and start accepting payments from their customers without having to ask anyone for permission. And, crucially, without having to trust any third parties. We want to build a future in which commerce is decentralized, permissionless, and censorship-resistant.

Payments processing for decentralized commerce

To achieve the above overarching goal, SolPayments is really a suite of smart contracts and related tools that form what we hope to be the base layer for accepting and processing payments on the Solana blockchain. To put it another way, our smart contracts are open and extensible. We provide basic payment processing and invite others to build on top of SolPayments.

Built on Solana

By utilizing Solana's tremendous speeds (currently 50,000+ transactions per second) and near zero transaction fees, SolPayments is able to offer an unparalleled experience where transactions are as fast as you’d want them, and cheaper than anywhere else.
